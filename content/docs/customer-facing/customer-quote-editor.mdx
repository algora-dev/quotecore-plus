# Customer quote editor

The customer quote editor is where you define exactly what your customer gets to see. You can use a [customer quote template](/docs/templates/customer-quote-templates) here, hide or show lines, edit component descriptions and prices, and create brand new quote items.

## Two editing modes

- **Fast edit (left side).** A show/hide system to toggle which lines, prices, and units appear on the customer quote.
- **Full edit (right side).** Full flexibility over the header, body, and footer of the quote.

## What you can do

- Apply a saved customer quote template.
- Hide or show individual lines.
- Hide the price on a line while keeping the line visible.
- Edit component names and descriptions for the customer view (without changing your master quote).
- Add new lines that exist only on the customer quote.
- Save, download, or print the customer quote.
