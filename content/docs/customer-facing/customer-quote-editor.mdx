# Customer quote editor

The customer quote editor is where you turn your master quote into the version your customer gets. Show or hide lines, edit descriptions, change wording, set prices visible or hidden. The numbers come from the master quote - you control how they're presented.

## When to use it

- Right after you save the master quote and want to send something to the customer.
- When you need to edit a customer quote before sending (or re-sending).
- When you want to add a custom line that's not in the master quote.

## Opening it

From the [quote summary](/docs/building-a-quote/quote-summary), click **Customer Quote**.

## What you see

The editor has two sides:

- **Left side: fast edit.** A list of every line. Each line has show/hide and price-visible/price-hidden toggles. Use this for quick clean-up.
- **Right side: full edit.** Click into any line and edit the description, the amount, or the layout. The full editor is where you craft the wording the customer reads.

You can switch a line from "show" to "hide" without deleting it - the line stays in your quote summary and labor sheet, it just doesn't appear on the customer document.

## Editing the header and footer

The header and footer come from your [customer quote template](/docs/templates/customer-quote-templates). You can pick a different template from the dropdown, or override the text on this specific quote.

If you keep editing the same things on every quote, save those edits as a new template instead.

## Adding a custom line

Sometimes you missed something or want to add a one-off note. Click **Add Line** in the editor. Give it a description, an amount (or zero if it's a note line), and decide whether to show the price. The line lives only on this customer quote - it doesn't go back into the master quote.

## Saving and downloading

- **Save** - saves the customer quote. You need to save before you can send it.
- **Download** - exports a PDF you can attach manually if you don't want to use the URL system.
- **Print** - prints the visible version.

## Tips

- Hide lines you have for your own records (internal markups, notes) by default.
- Use price-hidden lines for items where you want the customer to see what's included but the cost is bundled into another line.
- The customer quote re-renders any time the master quote changes. If you edit the builder, come back here to make sure nothing got out of sync.

<Callout type="warning">
Saving a customer quote does not send it. Saving just creates the draft. To get the URL or email out to the customer, go back to the summary and click **Send Quote**. See [Sending a quote](/docs/customer-facing/sending-a-quote).
</Callout>
