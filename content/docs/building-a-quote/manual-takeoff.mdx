# Manual takeoff (roofing)

Similar to the manual quote editor, but designed to use and add your saved components with your own measurements for each one. You add as many components and extras as you need, and then you can fully edit the customer quote and labor sheet once created.

This suits users who can't or prefer not to measure their roof or job with the digital takeoff system. It is also for users who have done a site measure or have **actual** measurements that do not require pitch calculations.

## When to use it

- You measured the roof on site already.
- You have a paper plan and prefer to enter the numbers by hand.
- You are quoting a re-roof and the measurements are real.
- You want the option to bypass auto pitch calculations on certain components.

## Plan vs Actual

You can flip individual components between **Plan** and **Actual** in the quote builder. Plan applies pitch and waste; Actual assumes you have already accounted for pitch yourself or measured the real length. See [Plan vs Actual](/docs/concepts/plan-vs-actual).

This is especially useful for re-roofs, or when you want to do a proper site measure after the initial plan-based quote was accepted.
