# Manual quote (all trades)

The generic quote creator. It lets you build a quote either using premade components, or as a completely manual line-by-line quote with no takeoff or pitch maths.

You can use your templates, or you can create the entire quote from scratch. This is the route for any trade that wants to build a professional quote from existing measurements and pricing, and put it into a quote they can quickly send and track with their customer.

## When to use it

- Trades that do not need digital takeoff (electrical, plumbing, joinery, fit-out).
- Quoting from existing measurements you already have.
- A one-off quote that does not fit your component library.

## Quote templates

You can save your own manual quote templates, letting you prefill fully custom items that are not in your components library. See [Quote templates](/docs/templates/quote-templates).
