# Quote summary

The quote summary is your master view of a quote. Everything you added is here, with margins applied and totals calculated. The customer never sees this page - it's for your records.

## What you can do from here

- **Edit the quote** - drops you back into the [quote builder](/docs/building-a-quote/quote-builder) at the Areas phase.
- **Edit the quote name and reference** - quick rename without re-entering the builder.
- **Create the customer quote** - opens the [customer quote editor](/docs/customer-facing/customer-quote-editor).
- **Create the labor sheet** - opens the [labor sheet editor](/docs/labor-and-installers/labor-sheet-editor).
- **Clone the quote** - duplicate it as a new quote with a new customer name.
- **Download the summary** - as a PDF, including all line items.
- **Add files** - roof plans, photos, supporting docs. See [Quote files](/docs/files-and-storage/quote-files).
- **Send the quote** - generate a customer URL or send via email. Requires you to have created the customer quote first.
- **Withdraw the quote** - pull back a sent quote. See [Withdrawing a quote](/docs/customer-facing/withdrawing-a-quote).

## What you'll see on it

- Customer name, job reference, date created.
- Each area with its pitch and plan number.
- Each component with its quantity (after pitch and waste), rate, and total.
- Each extra.
- Material subtotal, labor subtotal, margins, taxes, grand total.
- A "Files" panel at the bottom for attached documents.

## When to clone

Cloning is faster than starting fresh when:

- You're quoting the same job for a different customer.
- You're quoting a similar job with minor changes.
- You messed up a quote and want a clean restart from your current numbers.

Clone, change the customer name, edit the lines that need changing, save.

## Tips

- The summary is your single source of truth. If totals don't match the customer quote, the summary is right - the customer quote got edited.
- You can re-enter the quote builder any time to fix numbers. The customer quote and labor sheet flag for review when the underlying quote changes.
- Files attached to a quote stay with the quote. They show up here and on the digital takeoff page.
