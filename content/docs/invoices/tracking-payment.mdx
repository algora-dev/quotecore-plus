# Tracking payment

Once an invoice is sent, QuoteCore+ tracks the full payment journey.

## Customer side

On the public invoice page the customer can:

- View all line items and totals.
- Copy the payment reference (QCP-INV-YYYY-NNNNNN) for bank transfers.
- Click **Pay Online** if you've set a payment link in your payment details.
- Click **Payment Sent** once they've paid — this opens a short form where they can leave a note.
- Click **Dispute Invoice** if they have a problem.

## What you see

When the customer acts, you get an in-app notification:

| Event | Alert you receive |
|---|---|
| Customer views the invoice | "Invoice Viewed" alert |
| Customer reports payment | "Payment Reported" alert |
| Customer disputes the invoice | "Invoice Disputed" alert |

## Confirming payment

When the status is **Payment Reported**, a green **Confirm Payment** button appears in the invoice editor. Click it, confirm in the dialog, and the invoice moves to **Paid**. That's the final state — the invoice is closed.

## Disputes

When a dispute is raised the invoice moves to **Disputed** and you get an alert with the customer's reason. Contact the customer to resolve, then manually update the status as needed.

## Cancelling an invoice

Click **Cancel Invoice** in the editor top bar. You'll see a confirmation dialog first. Once cancelled:
- The public invoice link stops working (customer sees "Invoice Not Found").
- The invoice is removed from active totals.
- Cancelled invoices can't be re-sent.

## Payment reference

Every invoice has a unique payment reference (e.g. QCP-INV-2026-000001). Ask customers to include this in their bank transfer description. It makes matching incoming payments to invoices instant.
