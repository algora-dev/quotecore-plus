# Creating an invoice

To create an invoice, go to **Invoices** in the top navigation and click **New Invoice**.

## Options

### Blank Invoice

Start fresh. You'll be asked for a customer name and optional email, then dropped straight into the invoice editor. Add your own line items from scratch or from your catalog.

### From a Quote

Picks up an existing quote and pre-populates the invoice for you:
- Customer name and branding fields auto-fill from the quote.
- Visible line items from the customer quote are imported as invoice lines.
- Your [invoice template](/docs/invoices/invoice-templates) defaults (payment details, footer, notes) are applied if one is saved.

Search by customer name or quote number to find the right quote.

### From a Job *(coming soon)*

Will link an invoice directly to a Job record so billing is tracked against the project.

## Invoice numbers

Invoice numbers are generated automatically in the format **INV-YYYY-NNNNNN** (e.g. INV-2026-000001). Each company has its own sequence. Numbers are never re-used.

A matching **Payment Reference** (QCP-INV-YYYY-NNNNNN) is generated at the same time. This is what you ask your customer to include when making a bank transfer so you can match the payment instantly.

## After creating

You land in the [invoice editor](/docs/invoices/invoice-editor) where you can add lines, adjust details, and set up payment instructions before sending.
