# Invoice editor

The invoice editor has a **left panel** for editing and a **live preview** on the right that shows exactly what your customer will see.

## Line items

Each line has a title, description, quantity, unit, and unit price. The line total is calculated automatically (qty × unit price).

### Adding lines

Click **Add Line Item** at the bottom of the Lines panel. Choose from:
- **Custom** — enter a title and price manually.
- **Catalog** — search your catalog library and import a row with a single click.
- **Component** — add from your component library.

### Editing lines

Click the **pencil icon** on any line to edit its title, description, quantity, unit, or unit price inline.

### Reordering lines

Use the **↑ / ↓ arrows** to move lines up or down.

## Show / hide toggles

Every line has four checkboxes that control what the customer sees:

| Toggle | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Show** | Displays or hides the entire line on the customer invoice |
| **Price** | Shows or hides the unit price and line total for that line |
| **Qty** | Shows or hides the quantity and unit |
| **Add $** | Includes or excludes the line from the invoice total |

> **Tip:** _Show_ and _Add $_ are independent. You can hide a line visually while still including it in the total — for example, a contingency fee that's built into the total but not itemised.

When **Show** is unchecked, the Price, Qty, and Add $ toggles are disabled (they have no effect on a hidden line).

## Details tab

- **Invoice Date** and **Due Date** — shown on the customer invoice.
- **Notes** — free text below the line items.
- **Terms** — payment terms, displayed below notes.
- **Business Details** — click Edit to update your company name, address, email, phone, logo, and footer for this invoice.
- **References** — invoice number and payment reference (read-only).
- **Payment Details** — bank account details shown in the Payment Instructions box on the customer invoice. Changes here only affect this invoice; to update defaults for future invoices, update your [invoice template](/docs/invoices/invoice-templates).

## Auto-save

The editor saves automatically after 2 seconds of inactivity. The **Save** button at the top right saves immediately without navigating away.

## Collapse panel

Click **«** in the panel header to collapse the left panel and get a full-width preview of the invoice.
