# Invoice templates

Invoice templates save your company branding and payment details so they auto-fill on every new invoice you create. Find them in **Resources → Invoice Templates**.

## What a template stores

- **Company details** — name, address, email, phone, logo, footer text
- **Payment details** — bank account name, bank name, account number, sort code, payment link
- **Default notes** — pre-filled notes for the customer (editable per invoice)
- **Default terms** — payment terms text (editable per invoice)

## Creating a template

1. Go to **Resources → Invoice Templates**.
2. Click **New Template**.
3. Fill in your company branding and payment details.
4. Click **Save Template**.

## Using a template

When you [create an invoice](/docs/invoices/create-an-invoice) you can choose a saved template. The template's branding and payment details pre-fill the invoice. You can still edit everything per invoice — the template just saves you from typing the same details each time.

## Multiple templates

You can create more than one template — useful if you trade under different names, have different bank accounts for different types of work, or want a template with different default terms.

## Editing payment details on an invoice

You can override the payment details on any individual invoice from the **Details** tab in the invoice editor. Changes there only apply to that invoice. To change your defaults, edit the template in Resources.
