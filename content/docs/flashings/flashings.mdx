# Flashings

Two ways to add a flashing in QuoteCore+: draw one with the built-in editor, or upload an existing image. Either becomes a re-usable asset you can attach to components and material orders.

<Callout type="note">
On non-roofing trades, **Flashings** is your **Drawings & Images** library. The same tool — draw or upload any technical drawing or image you want to attach to components or material orders.
</Callout>

<Callout type="note">
Flashings is a **paid feature**, available on **Professional and above**. The Starter and Growth plans don't include it - the Drawings & Images link on the components page will open an upgrade prompt instead of routing through. The 14-day trial includes flashings with a small cap.
</Callout>

## Tier limits

| Plan | Drawings & Images (lifetime) |
|---|---|
| Trial | 5 |
| Starter | Not available |
| Growth | Not available |
| Professional | 10 |
| Pro Plus | 20 |

The cap is **lifetime**, not monthly. To make room for new drawings or images you have to delete old ones. Editing an existing one doesn't consume a new slot.

For full cap mechanics, see [Tier limits](/docs/account/tier-limits).

## Draw a flashing

Our flashing drawing system is great for creating clean, re-usable flashing drawings (or anything else you need) that you can attach to specific components either for your own reference, or to attach to material order forms.

You can draw all sorts of shapes, apply angles and measurements to them, and add custom values that allow flexibility later. You can also calculate complex angles like change-of-pitch flashings, multi-angle flashings, and other non-standard shapes. The purpose is to speed up the process, and create a clean and easy-to-read format you can send to suppliers. The result: fewer mistakes from messy handwriting, and a structured format suppliers like to see.

You can also print or download these drawings. Once you have all the main flashings or images you use regularly saved, this system becomes invaluable as a time saver and reduces the chance of flashings or items turning up on site wrong.

To draw one, go to **Drawings & Images > Create**.

## Angle Calculator

The built-in angle calculator helps you work out complex roof angles without leaving the drawing editor. Click **Auto-Calculate** in the toolbar to open the calculator widget — a draggable floating panel that stays open while you work.

Three calculation modes:

- **Rafter Pitch** — enter the roof pitch in degrees to get the rafter angle.
- **Hip/Valley Pitch** — enter the roof pitch to get the hip or valley angle.
- **Change of Pitch** — enter an upper and lower roof pitch to get the transition angle between two roof slopes running in the same direction.

After clicking **Calculate**, the panel scrolls to show the results. Click **Apply Angle** to push the finished angle straight onto the selected measurement.

The same calculator widget is available in the order editor — the panel works identically in both places.

## Upload a flashing

You can also upload a flashing or image that can be assigned to components or used in material orders. Uploading your own images gives you more flexibility if you have custom items you want available in orders, or if you do not want to use the drawing system.

To upload one, go to **Drawings & Images > Upload**.

## What you can do with each flashing

- **Download** the flashing as an image file.
- **Print** the flashing.
- **Delete** it from your library.
- **Attach** it to a component (so it auto-appears on material orders that include that component).

## At the cap

When you've used your allowance, the **Create** and **Upload** buttons open an upgrade modal pointing at the higher tier. Existing drawings and images are never deleted - caps only block new additions.
