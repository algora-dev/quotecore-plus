# Creating a component

Walk through it once and you'll have the pattern for every component you'll ever add. Most components take 30 seconds to set up.

## When to use it

- First time setting up the app - add the materials and labor you use on most jobs.
- Adding a new product or new supplier.
- Splitting an existing component into variants (different colors, gauges, profiles).

## Steps

1. Go to **Components** from the workspace nav.
2. Click **+ Add Component**.
3. Fill in the form (see field details below).
4. Save.

That's it. The component is now available in every quote you build from this point on.

## Walked example: roofing tiles

1. **Name:** Roofing tiles - clay terracotta.
2. **Type:** Main.
3. **Measurement:** Area (m2 or Rs depending on your default).
4. **Material price:** the cost per m2 your supplier charges you.
5. **Labor price:** what you pay your fixers, or what you charge for labor, per m2.
6. **Waste:** Percentage, 5%.
7. **Pitch:** On, Rafter pitch.
8. **Material orders:** Yes.
9. Save.

That tile component is now ready to drop into any quote. Set the area pitch in the quote builder and the maths is done.

## Walked example: ridge cap

1. **Name:** Ridge cap - 240 standard.
2. **Type:** Main.
3. **Measurement:** Lineal.
4. **Material price:** per linear meter.
5. **Labor price:** per linear meter.
6. **Waste:** Fixed, 100mm per length.
7. **Pitch:** Off (ridges sit horizontally, no pitch factor needed).
8. **Material orders:** Yes. Attach a flashing drawing if you have one for this profile.
9. Save.

## Walked example: skip hire (Extra)

1. **Name:** Skip hire.
2. **Type:** Extra.
3. **Measurement:** Quantity.
4. **Material price:** flat amount per skip.
5. **Labor price:** 0.
6. **Waste:** Off.
7. **Pitch:** Off.
8. **Material orders:** No.
9. Save.

## Tips

- Use specific names. "Roofing iron - corrugate 0.55 colorsteel" is better than "Iron".
- If you sell something in two finishes (galvanised vs colorsteel), make two components.
- Set waste defaults that are realistic for *your* installs. Don't copy someone else's number - they cut differently.

<Callout type="warning">
Pitch type can't be added later in the quote builder - it has to be set on the component itself. If you forget to enable rafter pitch on a roofing material, you'll under-order. Double-check before saving.
</Callout>

## What happens after you save

- The component is in the dropdown when you add components in [Manual takeoff](/docs/building-a-quote/manual-takeoff) or [Digital takeoff](/docs/building-a-quote/digital-takeoff).
- It's available to load via [Quote templates](/docs/templates/quote-templates).
- You can edit or delete it any time. Editing doesn't change quotes you've already built.
