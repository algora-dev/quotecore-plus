# Team

<ComingSoon />

Coming soon we have teams, which will let you add team members (by email) to your account and gate what they can and can't do in the app.

This lets you be the master account - create components, set templates, and manage everything - and then allow team members access to either:

- Just the components and templates you have already created, or
- The freedom to create their own components and templates too.

Watch the [changelog](/docs/help/changelog) for the release.
