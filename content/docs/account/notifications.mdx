# Notifications

Two things live here: email notifications for app alerts, and the Copilot visibility setting.

## Email alerts

Turn on or off email notifications for things like quote accepted, declined, or change requests.

<Callout type="note">
You will still receive important security-related emails (password changes, 2FA prompts, email changes) regardless of whether this setting is on or off.
</Callout>

## Copilot

You can turn Copilot on or off so that its toggle is selectable from the main view next to your Account and Logout buttons in the top right. You can change this any time.

If you switch Copilot off entirely here, the toggle disappears from the header. If you switch it on, the toggle is back and you can pause or resume Copilot guides from the header.

See [Copilot](/docs/help/copilot) for what Copilot actually does.

## Where to find it

**Account > Notifications**.
