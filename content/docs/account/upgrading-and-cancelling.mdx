# Upgrading and cancelling

All plan changes happen on **Account > Billing**. Stripe handles payment, card storage, and invoicing. You never enter card details into QuoteCore+ itself.

## First-time subscribe (new account, no card on file)

<Steps>

1. Open **Account > Billing**.
2. In the **Plans** grid, find the tier you want and click **Choose &lt;plan&gt;** - or click **View** first to see the full feature list, then **Purchase** from inside the modal.
3. You're sent to a Stripe-hosted checkout page on `checkout.stripe.com`.
4. The page shows the launch discount applied:
   - Subtotal: original price (e.g. $60.00)
   - Discount: −$X.XX (launch discount, applied automatically)
   - Total: launch price (e.g. $29.00/month)
5. Enter card details and confirm. Stripe charges you immediately.
6. You're redirected back to **Account > Billing** with a green "Subscription started" banner.
7. Within a few seconds the current-plan card updates to your new tier and your status changes to **active**.

</Steps>

<Callout type="tip">
The launch discount is locked in for the lifetime of your subscription. As long as you stay subscribed, renewals continue at the launch price, not the full original price.
</Callout>

## Switching between paid plans

If you already have an active subscription and want to move to a different paid tier:

<Steps>

1. Open **Account > Billing**.
2. Click **Manage subscription** on the current-plan card. You're sent to the Stripe customer portal.
3. Click **Update plan** in the portal.
4. Pick the new plan.
5. The portal shows the proration - either a partial charge (if upgrading) or a credit applied to your next invoice (if downgrading).
6. Confirm. You're back in QuoteCore+ within seconds with the new plan active.

</Steps>

<Callout type="note">
The "Choose &lt;plan&gt;" buttons on the plan grid are disabled for active paying customers (they'd create a second subscription, doubling your bill). The buttons say "Manage to switch" to point you at the portal.
</Callout>

## Cancelling a paid subscription

<Steps>

1. Open **Account > Billing**.
2. Click **Manage subscription** on the current-plan card.
3. In the portal, click **Cancel subscription**.
4. Confirm. You're brought back to QuoteCore+.

</Steps>

After you cancel:

- The status badge on your current-plan card may change to reflect the wind-down.
- You **keep paid features** until the end of the current billing period - you've paid for it, you get to use it.
- At the period end, Stripe deletes the subscription and our webhook collapses your effective plan to **Free**.
- Your data is **never deleted** on cancellation.

## Reactivating a cancelled subscription

You can resubscribe any time:

- **Before the period end** (sub is winding down): use **Manage subscription** in the portal to "renew" or "resume". Stripe will remove the cancellation flag.
- **After the period end** (sub is fully gone): from **Account > Billing**, pick any paid plan and click **Choose / Purchase**. Stripe checkout opens. New subscription, same launch pricing.

<Callout type="note">
You can't re-trial after subscribing. The 14-day trial is a once-per-account offer. The Trial card on the billing page will show "Trial unavailable" for anyone who's ever had a Stripe customer record.
</Callout>

## Updating your card

In the portal:

1. Click **Manage subscription** on the billing page.
2. Click **Update payment method**.
3. Add a new card. The old card stays on file until you delete it.

Cards are never stored in QuoteCore+. They live on Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure.

## Downloading invoices

In the portal:

1. Click **Manage subscription** on the billing page.
2. Scroll to **Billing history**.
3. Click any invoice to download it as a PDF.

Invoices are also emailed to the address on your Stripe customer record after each successful payment.

## What if my payment fails?

Stripe automatically retries failed cards on a schedule (typically day 0, 3, 5, 7 after failure). During this time:

- Your **status** moves to `past_due` then `grace`.
- You keep **read access** to everything.
- New mutations (creating quotes, sending orders) are blocked in `grace`.
- A banner on the billing page tells you what to do.

Update your card in the portal as soon as you can. The moment a payment succeeds, your status returns to `active`.

If 24 days pass with no successful payment, the subscription moves to `suspended`. After 75 days, `pending_data_purge`. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing#payment-failures-and-dunning) for the full dunning timeline.

## Disputes (chargebacks)

If you dispute a charge with your bank:

- We're notified automatically. A **support ticket** is opened on your account.
- Your subscription status becomes `disputed` while the bank reviews.
- When the dispute closes, the ticket auto-resolves and your status returns to normal (or, if the chargeback succeeded, your subscription is cancelled).

For non-dispute refund requests, [contact support](/docs/help/support-and-contact) first.

## Where to find it

**Account > Billing** for everything. **Manage subscription** opens Stripe's hosted portal.
