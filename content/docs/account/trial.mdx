# Free trial

QuoteCore+ comes with a **14-day free trial** so you can try the entire product before paying. No card required.

## What's included

Every feature is unlocked on the trial:

- Manual quotes, digital takeoff, blank quotes
- Customer-facing quote pages with accept / change / decline flow
- Email send from inside the app
- Flashing drawings and library
- Material orders to your suppliers
- Automated follow-ups
- Activity card on every quote

You get the **full Pro feature set** for 14 days.

## The catch: tiny caps

The trial has small numeric caps so you get a real feel for the product without using it as a free production tool:

| Cap | Limit |
|---|---|
| Quotes per month | 10 |
| Components (lifetime) | 10 |
| Flashings (lifetime) | 5 |
| Material orders per month | 5 |
| Storage | 100 MB |

When you hit a cap, an upgrade prompt appears. Existing data is never touched - caps only block new additions.

For the full breakdown of what each cap means, see [Tier limits](/docs/account/tier-limits).

## How to start the trial

<Steps>

1. Sign up for a new account.
2. Go to **Account > Billing**.
3. Click **Start 14-day trial** on the Trial card.
4. A blue banner confirms activation. Your current plan switches to Trial with a "14 days left" countdown.

</Steps>

No card details required. You won't be charged anything during the trial.

<Callout type="tip">
You can pick a paid plan at any point during the trial. There's no penalty for upgrading early - you just lose the days you didn't use.
</Callout>

## Trial countdown

While you're on the trial, the current-plan card on the billing page shows how many days you have left. The countdown updates in real time on each page load.

When you're down to **1 day** the message changes to make sure you notice.

When the countdown hits **0**, on the day the trial ends, the message says "Trial ends today - pick a plan to keep your data alive."

## What happens at the end

When `trial_ends_at` passes:

- Your **effective plan** collapses to **Starter**.
- Features above Starter lock - digital takeoff button greys out, flashings page shows the upgrade splash, material orders disappears from the nav.
- Your **data is preserved**. Components, flashings, quotes, customers - none of it is deleted.
- Storage limit drops to Starter's 200 MB. If you exceed it, uploads block but existing files remain accessible (subject to the long-term retention policy).

To resume full functionality you just pick a paid plan and your account upgrades immediately.

## One trial per company, forever

You can only activate the trial once per company. Once you've ever had a Stripe customer record (i.e. you've ever subscribed to a paid plan), the Trial card on the billing page changes to **"Trial unavailable"** and is disabled.

<Callout type="note">
This is by design: the trial is a taste of the product, not a way to avoid paying. If you signed up, used the trial, then paid for a few months and cancelled - you can't trial again. You can re-subscribe to any paid plan anytime.
</Callout>

## Frequently asked

**Can I extend the trial?**
No, but if you need a few extra days for a genuine reason, [contact support](/docs/help/support-and-contact).

**Will I be charged when the trial ends?**
No. The trial requires no card. When it ends you collapse to Starter (still works - just fewer features). You only pay if you actively pick a paid plan.

**Can I export my data if I don't want to continue?**
Yes - your quotes and customer data stay accessible. Each quote has a download option, and material orders can be exported from the orders page. For a full data export contact support.

**What if I started the trial then immediately decide to pay?**
Click any paid plan's **Choose** button. You go through Stripe checkout, the launch discount applies automatically, and your subscription starts on the spot.
