# Payment issues and storage

If a subscription payment doesn't go through, here's what happens and what you can do.

<Callout type="note">
The exact timeline below is the current plan. We'll publish the final policy before public launch.
</Callout>

## Timeline

- **Day 0:** Payment fails. We retry the card.
- **Days 1-60:** You'll get email and in-app alerts asking you to update payment details. Account stays fully active.
- **Days 61-90:** If no payment goes through, the account moves to a frozen state. You can log in to download quotes and files but can't create new ones.
- **Days 91+:** If we still haven't heard from you, file storage may be deleted. The quote text and line items remain longer.

## How to fix it

1. Go to **Account > Billing**.
2. Update your payment method.
3. Trigger a fresh payment.

If the issue is a billing dispute or you need help, contact support from the Billing page.

## Why files get cleaned up

Storing files costs us money. We can't store them forever for free. Giving you a clear timeline (with plenty of warning) is fairer than surprising you.

## How to back up before anything happens

- From the **Quotes page**, check the box next to a quote and click **Download**. You get a clean zip with the quote, customer quote, labor sheet, and all attached files.
- Do this for any quote you want to keep regardless of subscription status.

You can do bulk downloads if you want to archive everything before downgrading or pausing.

## Tips

- Keep your payment details up to date even if your card was working last week. Cards expire.
- If you're going on holiday or pausing the business, download quotes you might want before the trip.
- The 60-90 day window is generous on purpose. If we can reach you, we won't delete anything.
