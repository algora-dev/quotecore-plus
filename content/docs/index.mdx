# Welcome to QuoteCore+

QuoteCore+ is a quoting and job management app for trade businesses. It's built around roofing - measure off a plan, build a quote from re-usable components, send it to the customer, manage the job through to install - but most of it works for any trade that quotes by area, length, or item count.

This is the help library. Every screen and every feature has a page here.

## What you can do

- Build a quote from a digital plan, a paper measure-up, or a blank page.
- Save your materials and labor as components so you don't price them from scratch every time.
- Send the quote with a link the customer can accept, decline, or request changes on.
- Generate a labor sheet for your fixers from the same quote.
- Send clean material orders straight to your supplier.
- Keep all your quotes, files, and plans in one place.

## Where to start

If you're new, start with [Your first quote](/docs/getting-started/your-first-quote). It walks you through the whole flow in about five minutes.

If you've been using the app and you're stuck on a specific screen, the in-app help drawer (the question-mark icon at the top of any page) opens to the doc for whatever you're looking at.
