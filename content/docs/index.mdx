# What is QuoteCore+

QuoteCore+ is an end-to-end quoting and job management platform built to simplify the entire workflow for trade businesses. It is designed initially for the roofing industry and combines measuring, estimating, quoting, job tracking, and project management into one streamlined system, removing the need for multiple disconnected apps.

With QuoteCore+ you can create highly customisable quotes using reusable components, advanced measurement and calculation methods, material and labor pricing, associated drawings, and fully editable line items. The platform supports digital and manual measurements, multiple measurement unit options, auto calculations, tax controls, extras, custom products, and reusable templates that dramatically reduce quoting time while maintaining consistency and professionalism.

Our goal is simple: provide trade businesses with a single platform that manages everything from the first measurement to final installation. Whether you are creating customer-facing quotes, tracking projects, organising pricing structures, or preparing jobs for ordering and installation, QuoteCore+ centralises and simplifies the process.

Although optimised for roofing contractors, QuoteCore+ is flexible and adapts to other trades and industries. Whether you are a solo contractor or a growing company, QuoteCore+ delivers a clean, scalable, and professional workflow on multiple subscription tiers based on what tools and storage you need.
