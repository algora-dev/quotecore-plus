# Supplier templates

This section lets you create custom templates for different suppliers or requirements. The template covers the header and footer of material order forms and can be easily edited or applied to an order.

## When to use one

- You use multiple suppliers with different addresses or contact details.
- A supplier has specific ordering requirements (PO number format, delivery instructions, etc.).
- You want consistency on every order to a given supplier.

## Where to find it

**Material orders > Supplier templates**.
