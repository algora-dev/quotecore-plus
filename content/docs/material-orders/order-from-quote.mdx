# Order from a quote

This is the fast path. Pick an existing quote, the app pre-fills every component you've enabled for material orders into an order form. You tweak what's needed and send.

## When to use it

- The quote's been accepted and it's time to order materials.
- You're pre-ordering long-lead items before formal acceptance.
- You're building a partial order (just the roofing iron, you'll order flashings later).

## Steps

1. Go to **Material Orders > New Order**.
2. Pick **From Quote**.
3. Select the quote you want to order from.
4. Pick a [supplier template](/docs/material-orders/supplier-templates).
5. The order form pre-fills with every component that:
   - Was on the quote.
   - Has "Allow on material orders" enabled on the component.
6. Edit anything that needs changing - quantities, lines, drawings, layout.
7. Add or remove lines.
8. Save.
9. Download or share with your supplier.

## What gets pulled in

For each eligible component on the quote:

- Component name.
- Quantity (after pitch and waste maths).
- Per-area or per-length breakdown if relevant.
- Attached flashing drawing or image.

What doesn't get pulled in:

- Components without "Allow on material orders" enabled.
- Extras (usually - depends on the extra's setup).
- Anything you specifically marked as "not for ordering" on the component.

## Editing the order

You can do anything to the order after pre-fill:

- Add lines that weren't on the quote (extra fixings, accessories).
- Remove lines (if you're splitting the order across two suppliers).
- Change quantities (if you want to round up to standard pack sizes).
- Swap drawings or descriptions.
- Change the layout (single vs double column).
- Edit the header or footer.

## Tips

- Build the order well before install day. Suppliers appreciate notice.
- If you order from two suppliers, build two orders and split the components.
- Round up to pack quantities where it makes sense. Half a sheet doesn't get delivered.
