# Material orders overview

Material orders take what you quoted and turn it into a supplier order. Either build one from scratch, or pull straight from an existing quote and let the app pre-fill everything.

The point: stop re-typing orders on paper, stop sending sketches that get misread, get clean digital orders to your supplier with all the info they need.

## Two ways to start an order

### From a quote

The fast way. From an existing quote, every component you've enabled for material orders pre-fills into a new order form. Quantities, lengths, flashings, drawings - all there. Edit anything that needs changing, hit download or send.

See [Order from a quote](/docs/material-orders/order-from-quote).

### From scratch (custom order)

For one-offs or orders that don't tie to a quote. You build the line items yourself, attach drawings, set quantities. Useful for stock orders or replacement materials.

See [Custom orders](/docs/material-orders/custom-orders).

## What goes on an order

- Your company info (header).
- The supplier info (footer or header, depending on your template).
- Line items - component name, quantity, lengths, drawing or image if attached.
- Notes specific to this order (delivery date, site contact, special instructions).

## Supplier templates

Use [supplier templates](/docs/material-orders/supplier-templates) to set different headers and footers for different suppliers. Saves typing the supplier address and contact every time.

## Tips

- Get every regular component flagged for material orders. The "from quote" flow only includes components with that flag set.
- Attach a flashing drawing to every custom flashing component. Suppliers love drawings - they hate guessing.
- Send orders early. Even if the job's a few weeks out, getting the order in early gives suppliers room to shape it correctly.
