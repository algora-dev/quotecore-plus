# Custom orders

Custom orders are material orders that aren't tied to a specific quote. Build one when you're ordering stock, ordering replacement materials, or ordering for a job that doesn't have a quote in the system.

## When to use it

- Stock orders for materials you keep on hand.
- Replacement orders mid-job for breakages.
- Ordering for a small job where building a quote would be overkill.
- Pre-ordering long-lead items before a quote is finalised.

If you have a quote already, [Order from a quote](/docs/material-orders/order-from-quote) is faster.

## Steps

1. Go to **Material Orders** in the workspace nav.
2. Click **New Custom Order**.
3. Pick a [supplier template](/docs/material-orders/supplier-templates) for the header and footer. Or build from blank.
4. Add line items:
   - Pick saved components from your library, or
   - Add a free-form line with a description, quantity, and unit.
5. Attach flashing drawings or images where useful.
6. Set delivery details in the footer.
7. Save.
8. Download as a PDF, or copy the URL to share.

## Layout options

- **Single column** - line items run top-to-bottom. Good for simple orders.
- **Double column** - two columns, more compact. Good for orders with many lines.

You can switch layout any time on the order.

## Tips

- Save commonly-ordered stock items as components even if they're never on quotes - it makes custom orders faster.
- Use clear delivery notes in the footer. Suppliers care about the where and the when more than the what.
