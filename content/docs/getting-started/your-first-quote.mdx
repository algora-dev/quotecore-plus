# Your first quote

Start to finish: build a quote, generate the customer version, and send it. About five minutes if you've already added a couple of components.

## Before you start

- Make sure you've set up at least your **company name**, **default currency**, and **measurement system**. See [Set up your company](/docs/getting-started/set-up-your-company).
- Add a few **components** you'll use on most jobs - one main roofing material, ridges, valleys, fixings. See [Creating a component](/docs/components/creating-a-component).

## Steps

1. **Go to Quotes** and click **New Quote**.
2. **Pick how you'll build it.**
   - Manual quote (any trade) - line-by-line, no takeoff.
   - Manual takeoff (roofing) - components and your own measurements.
   - Digital takeoff (roofing) - measure straight off a plan.
3. **Enter the customer name and an optional job reference.** Pick a measurement system if you want to override your company default. Click **Create**.
4. **Add the work.** What this looks like depends on the entry mode:
   - Manual: type your line items in.
   - Manual takeoff: pick components, type areas and lengths.
   - Digital takeoff: calibrate the plan, draw your areas, draw your linear runs.
5. **Open the quote builder.** Areas first (set the pitch if it's a roof), components next, extras last, then review.
6. **Apply margins** on the review page if you didn't set defaults.
7. **Save.** The quote summary opens. This is your master view.
8. **Create the customer quote.** Click "Customer quote". Hide the lines you don't want shown, edit descriptions, save.
9. **Send the quote.** Generate a URL or send an email through QuoteCore+. The customer can accept, decline, or request changes from the link.

## What you'll have at the end

- A quote summary you can edit.
- A customer-facing quote with a unique link.
- A labor sheet you can build for your fixers.
- Status tracking on the quotes page (sent, accepted, declined, and so on).

## Tips

- Clone any quote that's similar to a new one - it's faster than starting fresh. From the summary page click **Clone**.
- If you missed a line item, add it in the customer quote editor without going back to the builder.
