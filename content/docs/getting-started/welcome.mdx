# Welcome

QuoteCore+ is a quoting and job management app. You measure, price, quote, send, and track jobs from one place.

## Who it's for

It's built for roofers, but most of it works for any trade that quotes by area, length, or item count. Cladders, flooring, fascia and gutter installers, foundations, builders quoting line-item work - the same flow applies.

You can run it solo or as a small company. If you're a one-person business it removes the pen, paper, and spreadsheet step. If you've got a couple of office staff, it gives you one place to find every quote and every plan.

## What it does

- Digital takeoff straight off a roof plan or aerial image.
- Manual takeoff if you measured on site.
- A re-usable component library so you don't price the same things again every quote.
- Templates for quotes, customer quotes, emails, and labor sheets.
- A customer quote your buyer accepts, declines, or requests changes on with one click.
- A labor sheet for your fixers built from the same quote.
- Clean material orders you can send straight to a supplier.
- File storage for plans, photos, and supporting docs.

## What it doesn't do (yet)

- Project management with schedules and dates - that's coming.
- Team accounts where you give staff their own login - that's coming too.
- Invoicing and payments - on the roadmap.
