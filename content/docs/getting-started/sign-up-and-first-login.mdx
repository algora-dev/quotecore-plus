# Sign up and first login

Create an account, confirm your email, set a password, and you're in. Most people are at the dashboard in under a minute.

## Steps

1. Go to the sign-up page from the QuoteCore+ home page.
2. Enter your name and email. Pick a strong password.
3. Open the confirmation email and click the link. If it didn't arrive in five minutes, check spam, then ask for a resend.
4. Log in. The app drops you on a short onboarding screen for your company name, currency, and default measurement units.
5. Save. You're now on the workspace dashboard.

## What you set up on first login

- **Company name.** This is what your customers see on quotes and emails.
- **Default currency.** Used on every quote unless you change it on a specific quote.
- **Default measurement system.** Metric, imperial feet, or imperial roofing squares. You can change this later for new quotes; existing quotes lock to the system you used when they were created.

You don't need to fill in your full company details on first login. You can come back to that later under [Company settings](/docs/account-and-billing/company-settings).

<Callout type="tip">
The email you sign up with becomes the contact email your customers see on the customer quote. If you want a different one shown to customers, change it in [Account details](/docs/account-and-billing/account-details) before you send your first quote.
</Callout>

## Troubleshooting

- **Confirmation email didn't arrive.** Check spam. Some company mail servers strip outbound links - if so, hit "resend" and try a personal email instead.
- **Password reset link expired.** Request another. Reset links are short-lived on purpose.
- **Account email change.** You can only change your account email every seven days. See [Security and 2FA](/docs/account-and-billing/security-and-2fa).
