# Message Center

The Message Center is a single inbox that gathers **every alert and message** across your quotes, orders, and invoices. Whenever a customer accepts a quote, a supplier responds to an order, or an invoice is viewed or paid, it lands here.

## Where to find it

Click the **orange envelope icon** in the top bar (between the alert bell and the Help button).

> **Envelope vs bell.** The **bell** is a quick peek at your most recent alerts. The **envelope** opens the full Message Center, where you can organise, search, and act on everything.

## Folders

The left panel has three folders:

- **Active** — your main list. This is where new alerts arrive and where the Message Center opens by default.
- **To-Do** — a "deal with it later" cluster. Push anything here that you want to come back to without losing it in your Active list.
- **Archived** — where items go when you mark them **Done**. Nothing is lost: you can **restore** an archived item back to Active, or **delete it permanently** from here.

## What you can do

Each message shows a colored type badge (Quote / Order / Invoice / Message), the title, a short preview, and the time it arrived. Unread items have an orange dot.

- **Open** — jumps straight to the related quote, order, or invoice.
- **Read** — clears the unread dot.
- **To-Do** — moves the item into the To-Do folder.
- **Done** — archives the item (moves it to Archived).
- **Restore / Delete** — from the Archived folder, send an item back to Active or remove it permanently.

## Search & filter

At the top of the list:

- **Search box** — type to match the text of any message title or body.
- **Type filters** — narrow the list to **Quotes, Orders, Invoices,** or **Messages**.

These work together with the folder you're viewing — e.g. show only **Order** messages inside your **To-Do** folder.

## Select several at once

Tick the checkbox on any message (or **Select all**) to select multiple items. A toolbar appears so you can **mark read**, move to **To-Do** or **Active**, mark **Done (Archive)**, or — in the Archived folder — **Delete permanently** in one go.
