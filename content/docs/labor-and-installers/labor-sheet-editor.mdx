# Labor sheet editor

The labor sheet editor is very similar to the [customer quote editor](/docs/customer-facing/customer-quote-editor), but you will only see the labor prices for each component or line item in the quote.

You can also edit or create the header and footer here, and download or print the labor sheet once saved.

## When to use it

- The job is accepted and you need to brief your fixers or installers.
- You want a separate document with no customer-facing markup, just the labor breakdown your team needs.

## What you can do

- Apply a saved [labor sheet template](/docs/templates/labor-sheet-templates) for the header and footer.
- Edit line descriptions for the team's view (without changing the customer quote or master summary).
- Save, download, or print.
