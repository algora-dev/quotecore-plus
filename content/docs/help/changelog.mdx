# Changelog

A running log of features added and significant changes.

## 2026-05-11

- **Docs rewrite.** Help library rebuilt from the master QuoteCore+ document. Tighter, fewer pages, no contradictions.
- **Account section.** Consolidated into one tabbed page (Account, Company, Security, Notifications, Billing, Support, Team). Tab switches are instant.
- **Support tickets.** New Support tab under Account: submit a ticket, see your previous tickets. Tickets get forwarded to our support inbox.
- **Help drawer.** Now a side-by-side panel - the app stays fully visible and interactive while you read help.
- **Bulk quote actions.** Limit of 25 quotes per batch download or delete.

## 2026-05-10

- **Flashings.** Download and print on every flashing in your library. Renamed Create / Upload buttons.
- **Quote summary tabs.** Stronger hover treatment on Customer Quote and Labor Sheet tabs.
