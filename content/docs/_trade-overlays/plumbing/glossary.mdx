# Glossary

Short definitions of the QuoteCore+ terms and plumbing measurement types you will see in the app.

## Plumbing measurement types

**Lineal.** Measures a single pipe run or drain line as a straight or jointed line on the plan. The primary measurement type for pipe work. Price is per metre.

**Multiple Pipe Runs.** Measures several individual runs on the plan and sums them into one total length. Use this when you want each run recorded separately but priced as a combined total.

**Curved Pipe Run.** Measures a curved pipe route or bend on the plan. The app calculates the true arc length from the curve you draw.

**Area.** Measures a floor or wall zone in m2. Use for waterproofing membrane, drainage mat, or any component priced by area.

**Volume.** Measures in m3. Use for tanks, concrete surrounds, trench backfill, or any component priced by volume.

**Count.** A quantity of items: taps, valves, toilets, basins, hot water units, floor wastes. Each point you place on the plan counts as one unit.

**Fixed.** A single flat price regardless of quantity. Used for call-out fees, permit costs, or inspection fees.

**Hours / Days.** Labor charged by the hour or day. Set a rate and enter hours in the quote builder.

## QuoteCore+ terms

**Actual measurement.** A real-world number entered directly - for example, a pipe run length measured on site. Waste still applies.

**Component.** A re-usable line you put in quotes. Has a name, measurement type, material price, labor price, and waste settings. See [Components overview](/docs/components/overview).

**Customer quote.** The version of the quote your customer sees. Built from the master quote summary in the [customer quote editor](/docs/customer-facing/customer-quote-editor).

**Digital takeoff.** Measuring directly off a digital floor plan or site drawing. See [Digital takeoff](/docs/building-a-quote/digital-takeoff).

**Extra.** A line you want priced and recorded but kept separate from the main components. Call-out fees, travel, permit costs. See [Components vs Extras](/docs/concepts/components-vs-extras).

**Manual quote.** A quote built line-by-line for jobs where you already have your run lengths and counts. See [Manual Quote](/docs/building-a-quote/manual-quote).

**Plan measurement.** A measurement taken from a floor plan or drawing. See [Plan vs Actual](/docs/concepts/plan-vs-actual).

**Quote builder.** The flow that builds a quote: Areas, Components, Extras, Review. See [Quote builder](/docs/building-a-quote/quote-builder).

**Takeoff.** The act of measuring a job - either digital (from a plan) or manual (on site or from your own notes).

## Billing terms

**Cap.** A numeric limit on something your plan allows - quotes per month, components total, storage bytes, etc. Hitting a cap blocks new additions but never deletes existing data. See [Tier limits](/docs/account/tier-limits).

**Dunning.** The process Stripe (and we) follow when a renewal payment fails: automatic card retries, status escalation through `past_due` - `grace` - `suspended`, with chances to recover by updating the card. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing#payment-failures-and-dunning).

**Effective plan.** What your account can actually use today. Usually the same as your purchased plan, but collapses to **Starter** during grace periods or after the trial ends.

**Grace period.** A 10-day window after a payment failure where you keep read access but can't create new quotes. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing#payment-failures-and-dunning).

**Trial.** A 14-day free taste of every feature, with tiny caps. One per account, ever. See [Free trial](/docs/account/trial).
