# Components overview

Components are the building blocks of every tiling quote. Each one bundles a measurement method, material price, labor price, and waste so you can drop it into a quote and let QuoteCore+ work out the quantities.

## What can be a component?

Anything you price more than once on a job:

- Floor tiles (area)
- Wall tiles — shower walls, splashbacks, full bathroom walls (wall length × height)
- Tile adhesive (area, packed by coverage rate)
- Grout (area, packed by coverage rate)
- Tile trim and edge profiles (lineal)
- Waterproofing membrane / tanking (area)
- Fittings — corner pieces, shower drains, niches (count)
- Fixed-price items like delivery, lift charges, or removal of existing tiles
- Hourly or day-rate labor

## Why components matter

Set them up once with the right pricing and waste, then every quote that uses them inherits those numbers. Change the price in one place and your next quote picks it up automatically. No more digging through old spreadsheets to remember what you charged last time.

## Extras vs Main components

**Main components** are the work you're quoting — floor tiles, wall tiles, adhesive, grout, trim. They're tied to an area and counted toward the job's measured total.

**Extras** are line items you want priced and shown but kept separate — removal of existing tiles, skip hire, disposal, parking. They appear in the quote total but not in the measured area breakdown.
