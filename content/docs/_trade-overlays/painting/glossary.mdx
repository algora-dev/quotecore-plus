# Glossary

Short definitions of the QuoteCore+ terms and painting measurement types you will see in the app.

## Painting measurement types

**Area.** A flat surface measured in m² - walls measured face-on, ceilings, large panels. Used for paint billed by coverage.

**Irregular Area.** An area that isn't a simple rectangle - raked walls, gable ends, bay reveals, multi-shape ceilings.

**Wall Length × Height (multi_lineal_lxh).** Several wall runs measured plan-view as lengths with a shared height applied. Fast way to quote whole-room walls.

**Wall Height × Length (length_x_height).** A single wall measured length × height. Used for one-off walls or feature walls.

**Multiple Trim Runs (multi_lineal).** Several skirting, architrave, or trim runs added together as one component line.

**Curved Trim Run (curved_line).** A curved skirting, arched architrave, or bay reveal measured along its curve.

**Lineal.** A single continuous run measured in metres - skirting, architrave, dado, picture rail.

**Count.** Individual items - doors, windows, radiators, shutters.

**Fixed.** A flat amount that doesn't scale with measurement - call-out, site protection, dust sheets, allowances.

**Hours / Days.** Labor billed by time - prep work, sanding, filling, supervision.

## QuoteCore+ terms

**Actual measurement.** The real-world measurement after waste has been applied to the plan measurement.

**Component.** A reusable painting item with its own price, coverage, labor rate, and waste settings.

**Customer quote.** The polished, customer-facing version of the quote. For painting work the default label is **Painting Works**.

**Digital takeoff.** Tracing measurements directly on an uploaded floor plan, elevation, or site drawing.

**Extra.** A non-core component bucket for call-outs, allowances, hidden items, or anything outside the main scope.

**Manual quote.** A quote built by typing measurements straight into the quote builder, without a digital takeoff.

**Plan measurement.** The raw measurement taken from the plan, before waste is applied.

**Quote builder.** The screen where you assemble areas, components, prices, and notes into the final quote.

**Takeoff.** The list of measurements that drives the quote - whether digital or manual.

## Billing terms

**Cap.** A numeric limit on something your plan allows - quotes per month, components total, storage bytes, etc. Hitting a cap blocks new additions but never deletes existing data. See [Tier limits](/docs/account/tier-limits).

**Dunning.** The process Stripe (and we) follow when a renewal payment fails: automatic card retries, status escalation through `past_due` - `grace` - `suspended`, with chances to recover by updating the card. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing#payment-failures-and-dunning).

**Effective plan.** What your account can actually use today. Usually the same as your purchased plan, but collapses to **Starter** during grace periods or after the trial ends.

**Grace period.** A 10-day window after a payment failure where you keep read access but can't create new quotes. See [Billing](/docs/account/billing#payment-failures-and-dunning).

**Trial.** A 14-day free taste of every feature, with tiny caps. One per account, ever. See [Free trial](/docs/account/trial).
