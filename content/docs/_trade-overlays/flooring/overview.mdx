# Components overview

Components are the building blocks of every flooring quote. Each one bundles a measurement method, material price, labor price, and waste so you can drop it into a quote and let QuoteCore+ work out the quantities.

## What can be a component?

Anything you price more than once on a job:

- Carpet, vinyl, LVT, laminate, engineered timber, tile (area)
- Underlay and acoustic membranes (area)
- Skirting boards and beading (lineal)
- Door bars and transition strips (lineal)
- Screed and self-levelling compound (volume)
- Door bars, vents, fixings (count)
- Fixed-price items like delivery, lift charges, or uplift of old flooring
- Hourly or day-rate labor

## Why components matter

Set them up once with the right pricing and waste, then every quote that uses them inherits those numbers. Change the price in one place and your next quote picks it up automatically. No more digging through old spreadsheets to remember what you charged last time.

## Extras vs Main components

**Main components** are the work you're quoting — flooring, underlay, skirting. They're tied to an area and counted toward the job's measured total.

**Extras** are line items you want priced and shown but kept separate — uplift of old flooring, furniture moving, disposal, parking. They appear in the quote total but not in the measured area breakdown.
