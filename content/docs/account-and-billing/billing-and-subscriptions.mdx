# Billing and subscriptions

The billing page is where you see what tier you're on, when the next payment is, and how to change subscriptions.

<Callout type="note">
This section is being finalised. Pricing and tier details will be published before public launch.
</Callout>

## What's on the page

- **Current tier** - the plan you're on.
- **Next payment date** - when the next charge is.
- **Payment amount** - what'll be charged.
- **Payment method** - the card or method on file.
- **Invoice history** - past charges with download links.
- **Upgrade / downgrade** - move tier any time.
- **Contact support** - for billing disputes or anything weird.

## Upgrading

1. Go to **Account > Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Pick the new tier.
4. Confirm. Charges are pro-rated where applicable.

The new tier takes effect immediately. Storage limits, feature access, and pricing all switch.

## Downgrading

Same flow as upgrading. Heads-up:

- Downgrading might mean less storage. Make sure you're under the new limit before downgrading or you'll have files that are read-only.
- Downgrading might disable features (follow-up emails, team accounts, and so on, depending on what's gated).

## Cancelling

You can cancel from the billing page. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period. After that:

- Your account moves to read-only.
- After the timeline outlined in [Payment issues and storage](/docs/files-and-storage/payment-issues), files may be cleaned up.

If you want to keep everything, download your quotes before cancelling. From the [quotes page](/docs/building-a-quote/quote-summary) you can bulk-download as a zip.

## Billing disputes

Contact support from the billing page. We'll look at the specific charge and sort it.

## Tips

- Keep your payment method up to date. Cards expire, payments get declined, you get locked out at the worst time.
- Download quotes before cancelling. It's much harder to get them back later.
