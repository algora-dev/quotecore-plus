# Notifications

The notifications page controls which alerts get emailed to you and whether the Copilot tutorial system is visible in the top nav.

## What you can control

### Email alerts

Toggle on or off for:

- **Quote accepted** - email when a customer accepts a quote.
- **Quote declined** - email when a customer declines.
- **Quote change request** - email when a customer requests changes.
- **Other quote alerts** - depending on what's enabled.

In-app alerts (the bell icon) always show regardless of email settings.

### Security emails

Always on. You can't turn off password change confirmations, 2FA setup notices, login-from-new-device alerts. These are critical.

### Copilot toggle

Copilot is the in-app guide that helps you through the first time you use a feature. The toggle here decides whether the Copilot switch is visible in the top-right of every page.

- **On** - the Copilot switch shows. You can pause and resume Copilot from any page.
- **Off** - the Copilot switch is hidden entirely. You can come back here to bring it back.

Most users leave it on. If you've used the app for a while and don't need the prompts, hide it for a cleaner header.

For more on what Copilot does, see [Copilot](/docs/help-and-support/copilot).

## Tips

- Turn email alerts on for **quote accepted** at minimum - you don't want to miss a job because you didn't check the app.
- If you're getting too many emails, turn down what you don't need. The in-app bell still shows everything.
