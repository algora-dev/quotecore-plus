# Account details

The account details page is for the person who owns the account - their name and the contact email. The fields here are personal to you. They're separate from your company info, which is what shows on quotes.

## When to use it

- First-time setup.
- Changing your contact email.
- Updating your name on the account.

## Steps

1. Go to **Account > Account**.
2. Edit name or email.
3. Save.

## What's on the page

- **Account owner name** - your name. Not shown to customers.
- **Account email** - the email you log in with. Customers see this on the customer quote as a way to contact you directly.
- **Email change** - email changes are limited to once every seven days as a security measure.

## Account email vs company email

These are different. Account email is the one you log in with. The "company email" shown on customer quotes is set under [Company settings](/docs/account-and-billing/company-settings) - if you don't set one there, customers see your account email by default.

If you want a different email shown to customers, set it on the company page.

<Callout type="warning">
Changing your account email kicks off a verification flow on the new address. Make sure you can access the new email before requesting the change.
</Callout>

## Tips

- Use a professional email if your account email is going to be visible on quotes.
- Set a separate company email under company settings if you want to keep account and customer-facing emails separate.
