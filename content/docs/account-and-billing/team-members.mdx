# Team members

<ComingSoon />

## What it'll do

Add other people to your account so they can log in with their own credentials and work on quotes alongside you. Owners control what each member can do - some users see everything, others only see specific quotes or specific features.

## Use cases we're targeting

- A small office where one person quotes and another sends and follows up.
- A foreman who needs read-only access to labor sheets without seeing pricing.
- An apprentice estimator who can build quotes but can't send them.

## What you'll set up

- Invite team members by email.
- Assign roles: owner, manager, member, view-only.
- Per-feature permissions: who can edit components, who can send quotes, who can manage billing.

## Status

Team accounts are on the roadmap. The auth and access-control systems they'll plug into are already built. We'll publish a release note when it's live.
