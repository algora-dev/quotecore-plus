# Contact support

Three places to look first, then how to reach us if you're still stuck.

## Try first

1. **The help library.** What you're reading now. Search at the top of the [docs](/docs) covers most questions.
2. **The in-app help drawer.** The question-mark icon in the top-right of any page opens the doc for whatever you're looking at.
3. **The [FAQ](/docs/help-and-support/faq).** Quick answers to the most common questions.

## When to email us

- Something is broken (a button does nothing, a quote total looks wrong, an email didn't send).
- You can't get into your account and the recovery flow isn't working.
- A billing question you can't answer from [Billing and subscriptions](/docs/account-and-billing/billing-and-subscriptions).
- Feature suggestions or feedback.

## How to email us

The fastest way is the support link inside the app - it pre-fills your account info so we can find you quickly. You'll find it under **Account > Billing** for billing issues and at the bottom of the help drawer for general queries.

If you're locked out and can't get to the in-app link, email the address shown on the login page.

## What to include

The more we know, the faster we can help. Useful things:

- What you were trying to do.
- What happened instead.
- Quote number, customer name, or screen URL where it went wrong.
- A screenshot if you can.
- The time it happened (roughly).

## Response times

We aim to respond within one business day. Faster on weekdays, slower on weekends and public holidays. If something's urgent (locked out before a customer meeting), say so in the subject line.

## What we can't help with

- Tax questions specific to your country - talk to your accountant.
- Pricing strategy ("am I charging enough?") - that's a business decision.
- Custom feature builds outside the standard product.

We can absolutely help with bugs, account issues, billing, "how do I do X", and feedback.
